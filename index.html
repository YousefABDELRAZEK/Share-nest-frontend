Product FAQs

How do I upload my files to Nestly?

+

Uploading files to Nestly is simple! Just click the "Upload" button on your dashboard, select the files you want to upload, and they’ll be securely stored in your account.

Can I collaborate with my team using Nestly?

+

Yes, Nestly is designed for teams of all sizes! You can share files, assign roles, and work together in real time to stay productive and organized.

Is my data secure on Nestly?

+

Your data security is our priority. Nestly uses advanced encryption protocols to ensure that your files are always protected and accessible only by you and your team.

Does Nestly offer mobile access?

+

Absolutely! You can access our app for both iOS and Android, giving you secure access to your files anytime, anywhere.

What happens if I run out of storage space?

+

If you run out of storage, you can easily upgrade your plan to get more space for your files. Check out our pricing page for details on available plans.

You can join our community or reach out to us if you have more questions. We’d love to help in any way we can!
